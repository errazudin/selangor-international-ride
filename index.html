Get Ready

25km Fun Ride

Training Tips

  • Consistency > Intensity: Aim for 2-3 short rides (30-45 mins) per week.
  • Practice Handling: Get comfortable with starting, stopping, and turning.
  • Go the Distance (Once): Try one longer ride of 15-20km a week before the event.

Nutrition Guide

  • Hydrate: Drink water before and during the ride. A single bottle is fine.
  • Light Breakfast: Eat a simple breakfast (e.g., toast, banana) 1-2 hours before.
  • Post-Ride: Have a small snack with protein and carbs after.

88km Fellowship Ride

Training Tips

  • Build Endurance: Aim for 3-4 rides per week, focusing on "saddle time".
  • The Long Ride: Gradually increase your weekend ride to 60-70km.
  • Pacing is Key: Practice riding at a steady, sustainable pace. Don't start too fast.
  • Hill Practice: Include some small hills in your training to build strength.

Nutrition Guide

  • Carb-Load: Eat a carbohydrate-rich meal (like pasta or rice) the night before.
  • On-Bike Fuel: Bring energy gels, bananas, or energy bars. Eat small amounts every 45-60 mins.
  • Electrolytes: Use sports drinks with electrolytes, not just plain water.
  • Recovery Meal: Have a meal rich in protein and carbs within 90 minutes after finishing.
